Weather Data: Measurements of weather conditions like temperature and rainfall
Temperature: Measurement of how hot or cold it is (in °C or °F)
Precipitation: Amount of rainfall or snowfall (usually in mm or inches)
Humidity: Amount of moisture in the air (as a percentage)
Time Series Data: Data points collected at regular time intervals
Seasonality: Regular patterns that repeat over time (like summer being warmer than winter)
Data Cleaning: Process of fixing or removing incorrect or missing data
Missing Values: Data points that weren't recorded or were lost
Average (Mean): The sum of values divided by the number of values
Maximum/Minimum: The highest and lowest values in a dataset
